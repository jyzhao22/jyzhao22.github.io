About me

(If you happened to discover this site, please bear for now that it is still under construction!)

My research centers around language representation and language comprehension in neural systems (human brain and artificial neural networks). Specifically, I investigate the computations and principles that underlie our ability of combining simplex concepts (as syntactic units) in a symbolic, hierarchical, and infinite manner into complex utterances (or, if this is actually real in the first place). I am also interested in how this fascinating mental capacity relates to other domains of cognition. To approach these questions, I use M/EEG, behavioral experiments and/or computational modelling.
